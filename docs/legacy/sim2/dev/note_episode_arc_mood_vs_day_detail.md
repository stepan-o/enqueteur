### EpisodeArcMood vs Day Detail — Clarification (v0.5)

EpisodeArcMood represents the episode-wide arc classification derived from the global delta across the entire tensionTrend (max minus min). It is a coarse, atmospheric read of the full span.

Day Detail and the Timeline Strip, by contrast, focus on local day-to-day direction (up/down/flat) between adjacent points. Both views are valid and complementary: the Mood Banner can signal “Building Pressure” (large span-wide delta) even if the Timeline shows a later easing trend day-to-day.
